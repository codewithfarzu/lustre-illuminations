About Lustre Illuminations

Being an ISO 9001-2015 certified organization, we aim to produce quality luminaires with minimal energy usage. Lustre Illuminations is well versed with latest manufacturing, quality control and selection techniques to produce energy efficient products.

We have over 30 years of experience in the lighting industry having catered to both govt. and private sector. We offer wide range of LED based lighting products (both outdoor and indoor) designed by a team of qualified lighting designers and engineers.

Lustre's LED based lighting products with their low energy consumption, high life expectancy and environment friendliness reaffirms our commitment to sustainable development.
